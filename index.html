About Miles Jennings

Miles Jennings is an entrepreneur and advisor with over 15 years of experience in technology and professional services. His expertise in digital marketing, strategic communication, and product design, combined with his strong passion for startups, has empowered him to forge a successful journey as a founder.

Miles is the President of CognoGroup and the CEO of Mediabistro. As the Founder of Recruiter.com, he led a bootstrapped startup into a Nasdaq-listed technology and services company serving clients in finance, healthcare, energy, and more.

He has led acquisitions and partnerships across HR tech, recruiting, and AI software, and serves on the Board of Directors of Nixxy, Inc. (Nasdaq: NIXX), and as Managing Director of a subsidiary.

He is the founder of the AI Exchange on LinkedIn, an advisor to Aura, and an All-Star mentor at Techstars. Contact Miles for questions and inquiries.

Publications

Miles has written for major publications including Entrepreneur.com, The Wall Street Journal, TechCrunch, Forbes, and the Hartford Business Journal. He has contributed to LinkedIn, Inc.com, Recruiter.com, Mediabistro, and Aura.
